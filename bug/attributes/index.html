Green attributes relate to how you can use the move.

Air OK
The move can also be done in the air.

Red attributes relate to how they affect the opponent when they are hit.

Launch
Launching attacks always knock the opponent into the air.

Blue attributes deal with special status effects or damage scaling.
All status effects expire either after a fixed amount of time (based on the move that inflicted it) or if the person who caused the status is hit.

IP and CP
Combo Proration is always added to the Combo Count when the attack hits the opponent. Initial Proration is added on top of that when the move is the first attack in a combo.
% min.
Moves with a % minimum cannot deal below the listed percentage of their base damage, even if damage scaling should make it lower than that percentage.
Freeze
Frozen opponents will be encased in a block of ice. After a short moment (or if the opponent is hit) the ice will break.
If the opponent is on the ground when the ice breaks, they will Crumple. If they are in the air, then they'll instead be launched into the air with a very slow fall speed.
Poison
When poisoned, a small amount of your health is converted into recoverable damage at regular intervals.
Burn
When burned, you will take an extra 10% unscaled damage from fire-based attacks.
Numb
When numbed, your speed is decreased, so you move and attack more slowly.
Ink
When inked, you are unable to use any Skills.
Heal
When the attack connects, you heal HP.
