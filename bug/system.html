system mechanics

Mobility options

Dash: Double-tap forward or press {{ D }} to start running forwards. You'll run as long as you hold forward or {{ D }}. You can cancel a dash into a crouch or block, or you can do dashing normals by pressing {{ B }} or {{ C }} during a dash.
Dash normals can't be chained into other normals, but can still cancel into Skills.

Backstep: Double-tap back or press {{ 4 }}{{ D }} to quickly step a short distance backwards. There is a small amount of invincibility at the start of a backstep.

Hop: Press {{ 6 }}{{ S }} to perform a short jump forwards. It lets you perform aerial attacks faster than a normal jump, but doesn't avoid grounded attacks as well. You can't chain or special cancel moves from a hop.

Super Jump: Quickly press down and then a jumping direction to perform a larger jump upwards.

Super Block

You can press {{ S }} while holding the stick in a blocking direction to perform a Super Block.
Successfully Super Blocking an attack will reduce blockstun and pushback, nullify chip damage, and give you extra SP and TP.
Additionally, Super Block is the only way to block some air-unblockable attacks.
Don't throw it out recklessly! Super Blocking locks you into an animation for a short period of time, leaving you vulnerable to throws or lows/overheads if you were blocking the wrong way.

{{ cornerR }}

Parry

Tap forward or down from a neutral position to initiate a parry attempt. This doesn't have an animation, but you will be locked out of parrying for a short amount of time afterwards.
If an attack connects within a short time of you tapping the proper direction, you will nullify all damage from the attack and be able to act before the opponent.
You can also perform a red parry by parrying in the middle of a blockstring. The timing is much tighter and failing it will cause you to get hit by the attack you were attempting to parry, so be careful.
Parrying prevents the opponent from chaining into normals, jump-canceling, and dash-canceling. However, they can still perform other cancels when applicable (like normal→Skill or Skill→EX Skill).

Burst

When the Burst meter is full, you can press {{ A }}{{ B }}{{ C }}{{ S }} during hitstun or blockstun to interrupt and perform a burst that knocks the opponent away if it hits.
The Burst meter refills from empty to full in about 60 - 80 seconds. The refill rate increases as your health gets lower. A successful connect recovers 30% of your Burst meter.
Burst startup is only vulnerable to GRABs, and the recovery of a burst is a counterhit state.
