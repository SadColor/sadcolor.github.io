Traits
Vi's Hop leaves her airborne and lets her act quickly.
Vi's Beeline gives her unique aerial mobility and cancel options.
Vi can also airdash downwards and diagonally downwards.
Vi is short and always takes crouching hitstun.
Strengths
High mobility with fast movement, more airdash directions, and Beeline.
Unique hop gives access to strong instant overheads.
Decent combination of speed, range, and disjoint.
Weaknesses
Eats up SP with high-cost Skills.
No meterless reversal.
Always takes crouching hitstun, so everyone can do longer combos on her.

Beeline
S

Vi readies herself then flies speedily towards the opponent.
When done with a backwards or vertical input, she will move slightly in that direction while readying.
You can do aerial attacks during the dive, and it can be steered to go higher, lower, shorter, or farther depending on your directional input.
You can cancel Skills into Beeline for an additional 3 SP.
