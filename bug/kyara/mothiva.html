Mothiva calls on Zasp to attack. You can Active Shift very early in the startup and Zasp will still appear.
S: Zasp does a moonsault kick that arcs downwards. It tracks to the opponent's position to some extent. In the air, Zasp always spawns at Mothiva's location.
S or S: Zasp darts in with a flying kick that travels fullscreen. The direction Zasp enters the screen and knocks the opponent is the same as the direction you press.
S: Zasp appears behind the opponent and tries to grab them. On a successful grab, Zasp kicks the opponent across the ground towards Mothiva. When done in a combo, the startup is much faster and aerial opponents can also be grabbed.
Other Skills can be canceled into a Zasp call.
Zasp will stop attacking if Mothiva is hit before Zasp can land a hit, or if Zasp himself is hit by an attack or projectile.
