Both versions are slightly plus on block when done as low as possible.
The A version is more plus, but leaves you further away.

Vi slashes with a needle while advancing.
An additional slash can be performed with A, which can lead one last followup.
Pressing A again has Vi leap and slash with both needles. It's fast and knocks the opponent far away. In the corner, it gives a wallbounce.
You can also press B, C, or B to go into B, C, or B, respectively.
On counterhit, you can combo into the B finisher and get a crouching combo afterwards.
The B finisher is always a low, even when done in the air. However, you have to land first before the slash will come out.
