Traits
Leif can freeze the opponent once per combo with certain Skills.
Strengths
Powerful ranged game with long normals and projectiles.

Normals are slow, high commitment, and hit specific ranges.
Low mobility.
Lacks good conversions from far away without resources.

Leif creates an icicle that launches straight forwards, or diagonally downwards in the air.
Using the air version bounces you backwards.
When passed through a Frost (S) cloud, the icicle will be fortified and gain an extra hit of unscaled damage that increases hitstun and blockstun. Fortified icicles also travel faster and redirect to the opponent's location.
Charged version is a large stationary icicle that hits three times and is dash- and jump- cancelable.
Used mostly on oki or as a greedy pressure reset.
Fortified charged version is still stationary, but increases in range with an additional final hit that deals extra hitstun/blockstun.
In Active Flow, you can chain uncharged 236A into 214A by pressing A.

Leif shoots an icicle diagonally downwards. When counterhitting an aerial opponent, it groundbounces.
When charged, the icicle ricochets off the ground. The groundbounce is very helpful in combos, or from converting off stray hits in neutral. The charged version can only be done on the ground.
When passed through a Frost (S) cloud, the icicle will be fortified and gain an extra hit of unscaled damage that increases hitstun and blockstun.
A fortified charged Icefall will redirect to the opponent's location.
In the air, it acts similarly to Ice Shard (j.236A), but pauses your aerial momentum briefly rather than bouncing back. The icicle launches on a delay.
In Active Flow, you can chain uncharged 214A into 236A by pressing A.
