Traits
Leif can freeze the opponent once per combo with certain Skills.
Strengths
Powerful ranged game with long normals and projectiles.
Freeze is powerful way to end combos into a mixup.
Can combo from normal throws anywhere for free.
Weaknesses
Normals are slow, high commitment, and hit specific ranges.
Low mobility.
Lacks good conversions from far away without resources.

Leif creates a frosty cloud in front of him that Freezes the opponent.
Not safe on block, but it's a useful combo-extending and reset tool.
The cloud lingers for a while after use, so if you set it up beforehand it can be useful as a meaty or to control space. The cloud can only Freeze during its earliest active frames.

Leif creates an icicle that aims at the opponent and then launches (doesn't home after the icicle launches).
Has a long startup before the icicle starts traveling (which is about the same time Leif recovers).
