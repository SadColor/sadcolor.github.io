Kabbu chops downwards. In the air, Kabbu's forward momentum stops and he dives nearly straight down.
The uncharged version is a very fast overhead, but you can't combo meterlessly, it scales as hard as a throw, and is unsafe on block.
The charged version is slower, but you can combo from it with lights (heavies if you counterhit) and it's safe on block.
The air version is good as an instant overhead and scales less harshly than the uncharged grounded version, but there are fewer opportunities to land it raw.
Air version can’t be charged.
In a juggle, all versions GROUNDSLIDE, allowing combo followups.
